Jerusalem Church of Christ is a very dedicated church to helping bring back the lost souls to God.
Baptism

Jerusalem Church of Christ baptize in faith of the trinity of God the Father, The Son Jesus Christ and The Holy Spirit.

Simplicity

Jerusalem is the only Church in Africa that embraces simplicity seen by sitting down in summon, use of simple drums, ‘’kengele,’’ whistle, kayamba and guitar in praise and worship

Discipline

Prophet Sinaida Mary set the foundation of Jerusalem Church on decent dressing among both gender, agape love and care.

Respect

Member congregants respect each other regardless of gender, age, race, profession or ethnic tribe.

Faith in God & Voluntary Servicing

Jerusalem Church has great faith in the Almighty God of Abraham, Moses and Mummy the founder of Jerusalem.
