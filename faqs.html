Answer: Mummy Mary Sinaida Dorcas was a revered spiritual leader, prophetess, and founder of the Jerusalem Church of Christ. She rose from humble beginnings, working as a maid, to becoming a beacon of faith and hope for millions. Known for her no-nonsense adherence to biblical teachings, she fearlessly spoke the truth, healing the sick, helping the poor, and spreading God’s word with unwavering conviction. Affectionately called "Mummy" or "Dada Mary" by her followers, she was celebrated for her powerful preaching, prophetic gifts, and her ability to connect deeply with those in need. Her ministry, which began in the late 1980s, flourished into a movement that touched lives far beyond her community in Kawangware. Mary Sinaida Dorcus will forever be remembered not just as a church leader but as a transformative figure whose life was a testimony of faith in action. Though she is no longer with us, her legacy and the spiritual foundation she built continue to inspire and guide countless beliefs.

The Jerusalem Church of Christ is rooted in unwavering faith in the Bible as the inspired Word of God. Our beliefs are centered on:
i. The Authority of Scripture
We believe in the Bible as the ultimate guide for faith and life, following its teachings word for word without compromise.
ii. Salvation Through Christ
Salvation is a gift from God, granted through faith in Jesus Christ as Lord and Savior.
iii. The Power of Prayer
Prayer is a cornerstone of our faith, connecting us to God’s grace, healing, and guidance.
iv. The Holy Spirit
We believe in the power and presence of the Holy Spirit to guide, empower, and comfort believers.
v. Compassion and Service
Inspired by Mummy Mary Sinaida Dorcus’ legacy, we are committed to helping the poor, the sick, and the marginalized as a demonstration of God’s love.
vi. Faith in Action
Our faith is not just a belief but a way of life. We strive to live in obedience to God’s word, reflecting His love and truth in all we do.
The Jerusalem Church of Christ welcomes everyone who seeks to grow spiritually, live faithfully, and experience the transformative power of God’s word.

The Jerusalem Church of Christ stands out as a beacon of unwavering faith and practical Christianity. Its uniqueness is rooted in:
i. Founding Legacy:
The church was founded in the late 1980s by Mummy Mary Sinaida Dorcus, a visionary leader who started her ministry while working as a maid. Her journey of faith and dedication inspired millions, making her a symbol of hope and perseverance.
ii. No-Nonsense Approach to Scripture:
Unlike many mainstream churches, the Jerusalem Church of Christ strictly adheres to the Bible's teachings without sugar coating or compromising the truth. This straightforward approach has drawn believers seeking authentic guidance.
iii. Focus on Healing and Deliverance:
The church is known for its emphasis on spiritual healing, prophetic revelations, and deliverance, offering hope and restoration to those in need.
iv. Community-Centered Ministry:
Inspired by Mummy Mary’s compassion, the church actively supports the poor, the sick, and the marginalized, ensuring faith is lived out through service and love.
v. Vibrant Worship and Fellowship:
The church fosters a strong sense of community through dynamic worship, uplifting prayers, and powerful preaching that resonates with people from all walks of life.
vi. A Legacy of Transformation:
Over the decades, the church has impacted millions of lives, not just spiritually but emotionally and socially, inspiring members to live out their faith in practical and meaningful ways.
The Jerusalem Church of Christ is more than a place of worship; it is a movement of faith, hope, and love that continues to touch hearts and transform lives.

Mary Sinaida Dorcus was more than just a spiritual leader; she was a cornerstone of her community. Her role extended beyond the pulpit, as she dedicated her life to uplifting those in need and serving as a source of hope and guidance.
i. Spiritual Leader:
As the founder of the Jerusalem Church of Christ, she provided a place of worship, healing, and deliverance for countless individuals. Her teachings, rooted in uncompromising biblical truths, inspired faith and transformation.
ii. Advocate for the Poor and Marginalized:
Mary Sinaida tirelessly worked to support the less fortunate, providing food, financial assistance, and emotional support to those in dire situations. Her compassion and generosity made her a beloved figure among the needy.
iii. Healer and Comforter:
Known for her powerful prayers and spiritual gifts, she brought healing and deliverance to many suffering from illness, despair, and spiritual struggles.
iv. Mentor and Guide:
She mentored individuals, encouraging them to lead lives of integrity, faith, and service. Her personal story of rising from humble beginnings to lead a global ministry was a source of inspiration.
v. Unifier:
Mary Sinaida was a unifying force, bringing people together regardless of their backgrounds. Her church became a family for many, fostering a sense of belonging and community.
Mary Sinaida’s role in the community was one of love, service, and unwavering faith. Her legacy continues to inspire and guide those she touched throughout her lifetime.
