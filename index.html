History

Get to know JCC

  • History
  • Mission
  • Vision
The Jerusalem Church of Christ (JCC) was founded in 1986 by Prophetess Sinaida Mary Dorcas Akatsa, known affectionately as "Mummy" for her loving and compassionate care. Her ministry focused on uplifting the less fortunate through acts of generosity, including feeding, clothing, and supporting people of all backgrounds. A strong spiritual leader, she was known for her prayers, prophecy, and healing practices. The JCC headquarters are in Kawangware, Nairobi, with additional branches in Bunyore, Kisa, Naitiri, Maragoli, and Mautuma.
